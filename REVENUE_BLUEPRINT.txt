LOPIPO REVENUE MODEL & BENCHMARKS

=== REVENUE STREAMS ANALYSIS ===

1. PRO SUBSCRIPTION (1 Pi/month)
   - Model: SaaS Recurring
   - Target: 10-20% of active users
   - Benefits: Unlimited polls, 2x daily spins, early access
   - MRR at 10k users: 1,000-2,000 Pi/month
   - Retention: High (daily benefits + exclusivity)
   - Psychology: Freemium → paid upgrade

2. FEATURED POLLS (5 Pi per 24h pin)
   - Model: Marketplace
   - Target: Brands, influencers, creators
   - Volume: 5-15 pins/day
   - Daily Revenue: 25-75 Pi
   - MRR: 750-2,250 Pi
   - Psychology: FOMO, visibility competition

3. GROUP LOTTERY FEE (5% of pot)
   - Model: Transaction-based
   - Triggers: Group pool payouts
   - Example: 100 Pi pot → 5 Pi fee
   - Scales with: TVL, user count, engagement
   - MRR at $10k TVL: 500-1,000 Pi
   - Psychology: Hidden fee, user still wins

4. AD BANNER (CPM/CPC)
   - Model: Performance-based
   - Target: Pi Network ecosystem partners
   - Typical: $0.50-2.00 CPM (crypto market rates)
   - MRR at 100k impressions: 50-200 Pi
   - Psychology: Non-intrusive, bottom-only placement

=== TOTAL REVENUE POTENTIAL ===
Conservative (10k active users):
- Subscription: 1,000 Pi/month (10% adoption)
- Featured polls: 750 Pi/month (10 pins/day)
- Group fee: 500 Pi/month

- TOTAL: 2,350 Pi/month (~$235 at $0.10/Pi)

Optimistic (50k active users):

- Group fee: 2,000 Pi/month
- Ads: 500 Pi/month
- TOTAL: 9,750 Pi/month (~$975 at $0.10/Pi)

=== BENCHMARK FEES (Pi Network Ecosystem) ===

Subscription Tiers:
- Free: Unlimited basic, 1 daily spin, 1 poll/day
- Pro: 1 Pi/month → Unlimited polls, 2 daily spins, early access
- Premium: 5 Pi/month → All Pro + priority support, leaderboard badge

Poll Pinning:
- Standard pin: 5 Pi for 24h (valid)
- Featured pin: 10 Pi for 48h (premium placement)
- VIP pin: 20 Pi for 7 days (top priority)

Group Lottery Fees:
- Solo lottery: 0% fee (keep 100%)
- Group lottery: 5% fee (platform takes 5%, group keeps 95%)
- Jackpot multiplier: 1.5x for groups (encourages participation)

Ad Rates:
- CPM (Cost Per 1000 impressions): 1-2 Pi
- CPC (Cost Per Click): 0.1-0.5 Pi
- Sponsored badge: 10 Pi/month

=== IMPLEMENTATION PRIORITY ===

Phase 1 (MVP): 
- Pro subscription ✓ Highest ROI, lowest risk
- Ad banner placement ✓ Already built

Phase 2 (Growth):
- Featured polls ✓ Unlock creator monetization
- Group lottery fee ✓ Scales with TVL

Phase 3 (Scale):
- Premium tiers ✓ Expansion
- Sponsorships ✓ Brand partnerships
- API access ✓ Developer ecosystem

=== USER PSYCHOLOGY PRICING ===

Why these numbers work:
- 1 Pi/month: Micro-commitment, low friction
- 5 Pi for pin: Feels like marketing spend, not gambling
- 5% fee on group: Imperceptible but adds up
- Ads: Free tier subsidies premium

Retention hooks:
- Daily spin bonus (FOMO for lapsed users)
- Leaderboard badges (status)
- Poll creation limits (upgrade friction)
- Early access to games (FOMO)
